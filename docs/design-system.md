### Titanio Automotive Supplies – Design System

#### Brand Essence
- **Identity**: Professional, bold, and engineered for performance.
- **Tone**: Industrial strength + modern polish.
- **Audience**: Auto body shops, detailers, and professionals in the automotive supply trade.

#### Logo Context
- **Primary Motif**: A sleek, custom blue truck set against a shield shape → represents strength, reliability, and shine.
- **Style**: Retro-modern with glossy highlights and metallic gradients.
- **Tagline**: “Engineered for Quality & Manufactured for Shine”

#### Color Palette
Core brand colors drawn from logo:
- **Titanio Blue**: `#0D4D9C` (primary accent)
- **Chrome Silver / Gradient**: `#D9D9D9` → `#999999` (metallic finish for UI highlights)
- **Jet Black**: `#000000` (background, strength, contrast)
- **High-Gloss White**: `#FFFFFF` (text and shine elements)
- **Deep Graphite Gray**: `#1C1C1C` (secondary backgrounds, neutral balance)

Optional accent colors (for calls-to-action / highlights):
- **Electric Blue**: `#009DFF` (CTA buttons, hover states)
- **Safety Red**: `#E50914` (alerts, urgency indicators)

#### Typography
- **Headings / Display**: Eurostile Extended or Orbitron → geometric, tech-industrial style.
- **Body Text**: Roboto or Montserrat → clean, modern sans-serif for readability.
- **Accents / Taglines**: Condensed or italicized variants to mimic motion/energy.

#### UI Components & Styling
- **Buttons**: Rounded-rectangles with metallic gradients or solid Titanio Blue. Hover state → chrome edge + glow.
- **Cards / Containers**: Dark backgrounds (graphite/black) with subtle chrome outlines or drop shadows.
- **Navbars / Headers**: Black or Titanio Blue background, chrome text or white highlights. Sticky top nav with bold uppercase links.
- **Forms / Inputs**: Minimal, dark with silver borders. Focus state → electric blue glow.
- **Icons**: Line-based, bold, metallic where possible (car parts, tools, detailing motifs).

#### Imagery Style
- **Photography**: High-contrast product photos with reflective shine. Close-ups of detailing, body shop tools, paint surfaces.
- **Illustrations**: Retro-modern vehicle silhouettes, chrome effects, blueprint-style linework.
- **Backgrounds**: Textured carbon fiber, brushed steel, matte black.

#### Layout Principles
- **Homepage**: Bold hero with Titanio truck illustration + tagline.
- **Shop Pages**: Grid-based, clean product cards with hover zoom.
- **Solutions / B2B**: Dark, professional tone with structured blocks.
- **Resources / Blog**: White or light-gray background for readability with automotive blueprint accents.

#### Voice & Messaging
- **Tone**: Confident, expert, performance-driven.
- **Guidelines**: Avoid fluff → emphasize quality, precision, and results. Consistently reference engineering, shine, durability, and professional-grade qualities.
